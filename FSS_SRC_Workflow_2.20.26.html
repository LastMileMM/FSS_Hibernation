1

Typical lead time: 3–7 business days. Travel billed at $99/hr. Multi-stop routes preferred.

🗺️
Direct vs. Mobilize: What DSPs Should Know
Direct stations (tech within 50 mi) typically schedule within 1–3 business days with tight, specific-day windows. Mobilize stations (travel required) have a 3–7 day lead time and wider windows — Kooner batches nearby stations into a single route to maximize efficiency. DSP flexibility on exact dates helps optimize scheduling.
